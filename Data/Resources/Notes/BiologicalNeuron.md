# Biological Neuron

The body relies on a **Central Nervous System (CNS)** which consists of two parts:
- brain (which has about 100 billion neurons)
- the spinal cord (which has about 50 million to 1 billion neurons)

These neurons are the fundamental building block of the CNS and we'll go into depth on the types of neurons and the components of the neuron.
